What We Do?
books@cafe is designed to be an oasis of freshness that brings people together in an
atmosphere of peace, love, and diversity. The continuing concept is to create a simple
open plan that combines a café and bookshop in order to integrate reading and to enrich knowledge in a social and colorful space over a cup of coffee, a tasty meal, or both.
